About Padmashree College

Padmashree College, established in 2001 A.D., is a premier educational institution located in Tinkune, Kathmandu. We are committed to providing quality education with a focus on practical skills and industry readiness.

Our college offers nationally and internationally recognized programs affiliated with Tribhuvan University (TU) and Nilai University, Malaysia. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a student-centric approach, we prepare our students for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Admissions

Admission Requirements

  • BIT/B.Tech: +2 or equivalent with minimum CGPA/percentage
  • BHM: +2 or equivalent from any stream
  • BCA: +2 with Mathematics or Computer Science
  • BBA: +2 or equivalent from any stream

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Character certificate
  • Passport size photographs
  • Copy of citizenship/ID
  • Migration certificate (if applicable)

Admission Process

  • Fill online/offline application form
  • Submit required documents
  • Appear for entrance examination (if required)
  • Attend interview/counseling
  • Complete admission formalities
  • Pay tuition fees
